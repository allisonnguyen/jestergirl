About

My name is Allison Nguyen, and I am a 22 year old game design student at BMCC. This website was created for the course MMP (multimedia programming) 100 and was taken as a part of the course requirements. I graduated from Purdue University with my Bachelor's in Computer Science with a focus on Machine Intelligence and Computer Graphics and a minor in Mathematics. However, I discovered at the end of my senior year that my true passion was in art and design. I decided to return to school to pursue game design, build my portfolio, and improve my design skills.
