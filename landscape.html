Landscape

Assignment

For this assignment, I needd to compose a landscape for my character to inhabit. The goal was to collect images based on the description of my character's origin and use Adobe Photoshop to create the composition using tools covered in class, like adjustment layers, filters, and masks. Additionally, the images needed to be in the public domain or have a Creative Commons license.
