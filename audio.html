Assignment

The goal for this assignment was to create a 1-3 minute long audio portrait that would describe my imaginary character's environment. The assignment required at least 5 tracks, one of which that was self-recorded, effects, and layering. Additionally, audio files must have a Creative Commons license or were recorded by myself.
